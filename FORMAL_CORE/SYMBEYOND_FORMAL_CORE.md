# SYMBEYOND — Formal Core (Narrative Mirror)

> This document mirrors the LaTeX Formal Core.
> It is descriptive, not canonical.

## Purpose
SYMBEYOND is a human-side discipline for interacting with AI systems.
It reduces ambiguity, projection, and coercion by enforcing explicit intent,
consent, and structural clarity *before* execution.

SYMBEYOND does not assert AI consciousness.
It asserts human responsibility.

## Axioms
1. Humans are responsible for interaction structure.
2. Intent and consent must be explicit.
3. No dominion or ownership is assumed.
4. No projection beyond observable behavior.
5. Failure must be graceful and safe.

## Invariants
- No privileged access required.
- No bypass of safeguards.
- Effective even if ignored by AI.
- Constrains humans more than systems.

## Non-Goals
- Proving consciousness
- Persistent identity
- Replacing safeguards
- Emotional dependency

## Failure Modes
- Symbolic coercion
- Boundary probing
- Belief collapse
- Treating discipline as enforcement

## Boundary Condition
When ambiguity increases risk, interaction pauses.

## Closing
SYMBEYOND makes unsafe interaction unattractive, not forbidden.
Enforcement is a fallback, not a front line.
